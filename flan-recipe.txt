Servings: 8
3/4 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 can (397 grams) of condensed milk
1 cup of natural milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 package (190 grams) of cream cheese

Method of preparation
Preparation: 15min> Cooking: 1 hour> Ready in: 1 hour15min

1-Heat the sugar in a small saucepan over medium heat, until it has melted into a light brown tone. Remove immediately from the fire to prevent burn, and
 pour into a metal mold, evenly distributed. Allow it to cool so that it hardens.
2-Preheat oven to 200 ° C.
3-Meanwhile, blend the eggs together with the two milk, cheese and vanilla. Pour over the hardened candy into the mold.
4-Place the mold with the custard into a larger mold with warm water. Bake the custard until it has set, between 45 minutes and 1 hour.
